Here’s all the things you can do in the Metrobank App!

Send money

Transfer funds from your deposit account—real time through InstaPay and PESONet.

Pay bills

Pay bills using your deposit account or credit card.

Send load

Stay connected when you buy mobile load for yourself or your loved ones.

PayNow

Convert big credit card purchases into monthly installments.
