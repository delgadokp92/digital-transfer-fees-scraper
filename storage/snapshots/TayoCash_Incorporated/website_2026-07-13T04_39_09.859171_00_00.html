What is Instapay?

InstaPay is an Electronic Fund Transfer service that allows you to transfer funds between your TayoCash e-wallet and another account in another participating BSP-supervised bank or and non-bank e-money issuer, like TayoCash, in the Philippines.

That means, you can now send and receive funds or make payments via QR PH REAL TIME, 24/7 using your TayoCash mobile app.

What is QR PH?

QR PH is the National QR Code Standard for digital fund transfers and payments using the Quick Response code technology of banks, non-bank electronic money issuers, like TayoCash, and participating merchants and partners.

This is through the initiative of the BSP to promote an interoperable common QR Code that can be scanned and accepted for quick and safe fund transfer and payment requests.
