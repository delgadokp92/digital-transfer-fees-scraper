Cash In

How do I put money in my TayoCash wallet?

You can Cash In or load your TayoCash Wallet through:

  1. Our TayoCash Partners and Merchants; or
  2. UnionBank - either by Over-The-Counter Deposit in any UnionBank branch or through the official UnionBank mobile application or online banking portal.

Cash In transactions are subject to applicable service fees.

How do I Cash In through a TayoCash Partner or Merchant?

  1. In the home page of your app, click the Cash In icon and enter the Cash In amount.
  2. Tap Select a Partner, Bank or Merchant to see the list of Partners or Merchants.
  3. Go to the Partner/Merchant and pay the Cash In amount. The Cash In amount, less the applicable service fee, will then be reflected in your TayoCash Wallet.

How can I make a Cash In through Over-The-Counter (OTC) bank deposit via UnionBank?

  1. In the home page of your app, click the Cash In icon and enter the Cash In amount.
  2. Tap the Select a Partner, Bank or Merchant tab.
  3. Tap UnionBank from the VIA BANK DEPOSIT option.
  4. Review the transaction details and take note of the TayoCash Transaction Reference Number. Tap Continue.
  5. Go to the nearest UnionBank Branch.
  6. At the UnionBank branch, use UnionBank’s tablet, and tap Start Here and select Bills Payment.
  7. Search for TayoCash under the list of UnionBank billers. Select TayoCash as the biller.
  8. Enter the TayoCash Transaction Reference Number shown in your TayoCash app.
  9. Enter your name, email address, and mobile number registered in your TayoCash account. Click OK.
  10. Review the details of your transaction.
  11. Choose a payment method.
  12. Wait for your name to be called by the bank teller and proceed with the payment of the Cash In amount and the bank service charge.
  13. Once you’re done, take a photo of the SMS/email confirmation from UnionBank (showing the UnionBank Transaction Reference Number) and upload it through the pop-up notification or the Mail icon in your TayoCash app to complete your Cash In request.
  14. Our Customer Care Representative will validate your Cash In transaction within 24 hours and once done, the amount you deposited, less any applicable service fee, will be reflected in your TayoCash Wallet.

How do I Cash In through the Pay Bills feature in my UnionBank app or online banking portal?

  1. In the home page of your TayoCash app, click the Cash In icon and enter the Cash In amount.
  2. Tap the Select a Partner, Bank or Merchant tab.
  3. Tap UnionBank from the VIA BANK DEPOSIT option.
  4. Review the transaction details and take note of the TayoCash Transaction Reference Number. Tap Continue.
  5. Open your UnionBank mobile app or online banking portal and select “Pay Bills”.
  6. Search for TayoCash under the list of UnionBank billers. Select TayoCash as the biller.
  7. Enter the TayoCash Transaction Reference Number shown in your TayoCash app and the Mobile Number registered in your TayoCash account.
  8. Enter the amount you want to Cash In.
  9. Review the summary of your transaction and confirm.
  10. Take a screenshot or photo of the Cash In Transaction Receipt (showing the UnionBank Transaction Reference Number) in your UnionBank app or online banking portal and upload it through the in-app notification or the Mail button in your TayoCash app to complete your Cash In request.
  11. Our Customer Care Representative will validate your Cash In transaction within 24 hours and once done, the Cash In amount, less any applicable service fee, will be reflected in your TayoCash wallet.

How do I Cash In from my UnionBank account linked to my TayoCash account?

  1. In the home page of your app, click the Cash In icon and enter the Cash In amount.
  2. Tap the Select a Partner, Bank or Merchant tab.
  3. Choose UnionBank from the list of online banks.
  4. The UnionBank authentication page will appear on your app screen.
  5. Log in your UnionBank account details.
  6. After log in, select the UnionBank account that you want to Cash In from.
  7. Confirm your Cash In request and enter the One-Time PIN (OTP) sent to your mobile number.
  8. The Cash In amount will be deducted from your UnionBank account and will be reflected in your TayoCash Wallet, less any applicable service fee.

I do not see the Cash In amount in my wallet, what should I do?

  1. For Cash In via a TayoCash Partner or Merchant:
    • Please make sure that all the details of the transaction are correct.
    • Confirm with the Partner or Merchant if the transaction was successful from their end and request for a transaction receipt if it is successful.
    • If the transaction was unsuccessful, ask the Partner or Merchant to try again.
  2. For Cash In via OTC bank deposit at any UnionBank branch or via the Pay Bills feature through the UnionBank app or online banking portal:
    • Kindly upload in your TayoCash app a copy of the Cash In transaction receipt or the SMS/email confirmation you received from UnionBank (showing the UnionBank Transaction Reference Number).
    • Please take note that transactions will be confirmed and credited to your TayoCash available wallet balance within 24 hours after uploading a copy of your Cash In transaction receipt or SMS/email confirmation in the TayoCash app. All Cash In requests submitted for validation after 4:00 PM shall begin processing the next business day.
  3. For Cash In via your UnionBank account linked to your TayoCash account:
    • Please make sure that all the details of the transaction are correct.
    • Confirm if your bank account was deducted with the Cash In amount.

I’m still experiencing the same problem or I have other concerns in using the Cash In Service. What do I do?

  1. Submit a ticket via the TayoCash Help Center in the app or website:
    • Select Cash In Transaction
    • Input the following details in the text field:
      • Cash In Channel (e.g. UnionBank, TayoCash Partner/Merchant)
      • Amount
      • Date and Time of Transaction
      • TayoCash Transaction Reference Number
      • UnionBank Transaction Reference number (for Cash In thru UnionBank channels)
  2. Send us an email at ccg@tayocash.com:
    • Email Subject: Cash In Transaction Issue
    • Email Content:
      • Cash In Channel (e.g. UnionBank, TayoCash Partner/Merchant)
      • Amount
      • Date and Time of Transaction
      • TayoCash Transaction Reference Number
      • UnionBank Transaction Reference Number (for Cash In thru UnionBank channels)
      • Transaction Dispute Form

A TayoCash Customer Care Representative will get back to you within 48 hours after you have submitted the ticket.

Cash Out

How to Cash Out?

  1. Log in to your TayoCash Account and select Cash Out.
  2. Select your preferred Cash Out Partner and check the instructions on how to Cash Out.
  3. Enter or select the amount that you would like to Cash Out.
  4. Review the completeness of the transaction details and enter the 6-digit OTP verification code.
  5. Save the Reference Code by tapping the “Save to Gallery” icon.
  6. Go to the nearest Partner outlet and fill out the “Payment Form.”
  7. Submit your accomplished “Payment Form” to the Teller along with a government-issued valid ID.
  8. You will receive an SMS notification that the Cash Out transaction has been completed.
  9. Remember to always count your money before leaving the outlet.

Where can I Cash Out?

Cash Out via TayoCash Partner outlets (Over-the-Counter).
You can do Over-The-Counter Cash Out transactions from any of our partner outlets.

How much is the Cash Out Fee?

The Cash Out fee is 1.25% of the total cash out amount. The fee will be charged with all Cash Out transactions regardless of amount/value.

How much can I Cash Out?

The amount you can Cash Out or withdraw depends on your TayoCash Membership wallet limit.
Click here to learn about the corresponding wallet limit for each TayoCash Membership type.

I can’t Cash Out, what do I do?

The Cash Out feature is only available for fully verified users. To learn how to get verified, click here

Can I ask someone to Cash Out on my behalf?

The person who owns the TayoCash account is the only one authorized to Cash Out. TayoCash and our partners do not accept third-party requests for Cash out transactions.

I received an SMS confirmation for an unauthorized Cash Out transaction, what should I do?

We're sorry to hear about this. To avoid further unauthorized use of your account, please change your MPIN immediately.

We will try our best to assist you in retrieving your funds, but please take note that we cannot guarantee anything.

Upon your request, we can temporarily suspend your account to prevent any further unauthorized transactions while we investigate the situation. To do so, please submit a ticket and include the following details:

  • Details of your concern
  • Your TayoCash Registered Name
  • Your TayoCash Registered Mobile Number
  • Date and time of transaction
  • Transaction Reference No.
  • Screenshots of the transaction (If available)

A TayoCash Customer Care Representative will get back to you as soon as possible after you have sent your inquiry.

What kind of ID do I need to bring?

For every Cash Out transaction, members must be ready to present a valid government- issued ID to the Teller.

Buy Load

How do I buy prepaid load and/or gaming credits using my TayoCash wallet?

  1. Open your TayoCash app and select Buy Load.
  2. Enter the Mobile Number you want to send load to or allow TayoCash to access your contacts so you may easily choose from your contact list.
  3. Enter the amount or select the load item/promo you wish to purchase and tap Next.
  4. You will be shown a summary of the purchase. Tap the Confirm button to proceed.
  5. You should instantly receive an SMS notification for your load purchase.

I purchased prepaid load and/or gaming credits but I have entered an incorrect amount. What do I do?

Unfortunately, we cannot refund load transactions once they have been processed. This is due to the agreement we have with our external merchants or partners. Please make sure to check if all your details are correct before proceeding with the buy load transaction.

I purchased prepaid load and/or gaming credits but I have entered an incorrect mobile number. What do I do?

Unfortunately, we cannot refund load transactions once they have been processed. This is due to the agreement we have with our external merchants or partners. Please make sure to check if all your details are correct before proceeding with the buy load transaction.

I got charged after buying prepaid load/gaming credits but did not receive the prepaid load in my mobile number. What do I do?

If you still did not receive the prepaid load, you may submit a ticket via the TayoCash Help Center in the app or website, or email us at ccg@tayocash.com.

Bank Transfers

How do I transfer funds to banks or other e-wallet accounts?

You may transfer funds to bank or other e-wallet accounts via InstaPay or PESONet by following the steps below:

  1. Open your TayoCash app and click Bank Transfer.
  2. Enter the amount you want to send.
  3. Scan or upload the QR PH code of the recipient OR manually input the transaction details.

    If you scan or upload the QR PH code, the details of the transaction will automatically be filled out in the TayoCash app.

    if you have no QR PH Code,

    • Select the Bank or E-wallet you want to send the funds to.
    • Choose between Instapay or PESONet and enter the recipient’s bank or e-wallet details (Account Name, Account Number and Email Address, as well as Purpose of Transaction).
  4. Click the Review button and check the details of the transaction. Once you’ve validated all information click Confirm.
  5. Upon the successful transfer, you may save the Transaction Receipt provided in the TayoCash app. You will also receive an SMS notification from TayoCash confirming the transaction.

The funds I transferred were not reflected in the recipient’s bank or e-wallet account, what should I do?

  1. Please make sure that all the details of the transaction are complete and correct.
  2. Confirm if your TayoCash wallet balance was correctly deducted.
  3. Submit a ticket via the TayoCash Help Center in the app or website:
    • Select Bank Transfer Transaction
    • Input the following details in the text field:
      • Bank Transfer via Instapay or PESONet
      • Amount
      • Date and Time of Transaction
      • TayoCash Transaction Reference Number

A TayoCash Customer Care Representative will get back to you within 48 hours after you have submitted the ticket.

The amount I transferred is incorrect. What should I do?

  1. If your fund transfer is less than the intended amount, kindly transact again using the TayoCash App.
  2. If your fund transfer is more than the intended amount, kindly coordinate with the recipient for the return of the excess amount.

I transferred funds to an incorrect account. What should I do?

  1. Reminder: Please make sure that all the details of the transaction are complete and correct.
  2. You may coordinate directly with the account holder for the amount to be returned to your account.
  3. Submit a ticket via the TayoCash Help Center in the app or website:
    • Select Fund Transfer Transaction
    • Input the following details in the text field:
      • Bank Transfer via Instapay or PESONet
      • Bank Name
      • Amount
      • Date and Time of Transaction
      • TayoCash Transaction Reference Number
      • Screenshot of the Issue (if available)

A TayoCash Customer Care Representative will extend any possible assistance.

I have other concerns in using the Fund Transfer Service. What should I do?

  1. Submit a ticket via the TayoCash Help Center in the app or website:
    • Select Fund Transfer Transaction
    • Input the following details in the text field:
      • Bank Transfer via Instapay or PESONet
      • Amount

What are the cut-off periods for PESONet transactions?

The following are the cut-off periods and corresponding crediting time for PESONet:

Cut-off CycleTimeCovered Transactions / Cut-off Schedule
110:00 AM- 4:00:01 PM to 00:00:00 AM transactions from the previous day
- 00:00:01 AM to 10:00:00 AM transactions of the current business day
24:00 PM- 10:00:01 AM to 4:00:00 PM transactions of the current day

PESONet is available during banking days. Transactions received after the 4PM cut-off, on holidays or weekends will be processed for transmission to the receiving bank on the next banking day.

Will there be instances for unsuccessful credit to the receiving bank?

The final credit to the beneficiary account may not be successfully processed by the receiving bank for several reasons, such as:

  1. Invalid beneficiary account number (may be incorrect, closed, dormant, blocked, frozen)
  2. The currency of the beneficiary account is not a qualified PHP account

Please ensure to obtain the correct account information to avoid delays in the credit to the beneficiary account.

Can I request for cancellation of the transaction?

Once your account has been debited, the transaction is considered final and can no longer be cancelled.

Pay QR

What is Pay QR?

Pay QR service is a contactless and cashless form of transaction that allows you to pay for our Merchants’ goods and/or services by scanning their assigned QR code using the TayoCash app.

How do I use the Pay QR PH feature?

You can use the Pay QR feature at any accredited TayoCash Merchant’s physical or online store.

  1. Open the app and press the Scan QR Icon found at the top right portion of your screen.
  2. Place the Merchant’s QR PH code inside the box.
  3. Enter the amount you need to pay and confirm.
  4. A transaction receipt will appear in your TayoCash app for the confirmed Pay QR PH transaction. Check your new wallet balance.
  5. You will receive an SMS confirmation/app notification from TayoCash confirming the transaction.

My Pay QR PH transaction is not going through. What should I do?

If you are having trouble scanning a Pay QR PH code, try the following:

  • Ensure that your camera lens is clear and that the whole QR PH code is inside the frame of your phone's camera/scanner.
  • Check if you have a stable mobile internet connection.
  • Check if your TayoCash app software is updated.
  • You may also try restarting the app and your phone.
  • If none of the above worked, you may Submit a ticket via the Help Center in the TayoCash app or website, or email TayoCash Customer Care Group at ccg@tayocash.com.

I just made a Pay QR PH transaction, the TayoCash Merchant did not receive a confirmation of my QR payment. What should I do?

Please check your TayoCash Wallet:

  • If it has been deducted for the transaction, there might just be a short delay in the SMS confirmation. Please check your mobile network connection. After a few minutes, you may ask the TayoCash Merchant to contact us so we can help.
  • If your TayoCash wallet was not deducted, you may try processing the payment again.

I have put in an amount more than I intended to pay on my Pay QR PH transaction. What should I do?

If you have put in an amount more than what is required, you can ask the TayoCash Merchant for settlement. If the Merchant refuses, you may file a Tayocash Dispute Form via the Help Center.
