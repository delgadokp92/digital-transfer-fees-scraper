MAKE MULTIPLE FUND TRANSFER TRANSACTIONS VIA PESONET

PESONet (Philippine EFT System and Operations Network) allows you to make fund transfers to other financial institutions. PESONet fund transfer maximum transaction limit for the day in AUB's Online and Mobile Banking depends on the client's Online Banking limit.

Fund transfer fee is Php 8* per transaction. Crediting to account depends on the cut-off processing time(s) of the Receiving Bank.

This feature is only available through AUB Preferred Online Banking and Mobile Banking. Currently unavailable via HelloMoney and Hello Pag-IBIG

*Fees are subject to change.

WHY USE PESONet?

SAME DAY CREDITING

LOW TRANSACTION FEES

CONVENIENT & SECURED

NO MORE WAITING LINES

You may now transfer your funds to other financial institutions via PESONet using your AUB Preferred Online and Mobile Banking accounts. Do it anytime and anywhere without visiting the bank.

SAME-DAY CREDITING OF FUNDS

Funds you send within the cut-off period will be credited within the same banking day. For fund transfers made after the cut-off time, transaction will be credited the next banking day.

AFFORDABLE AND FLEXIBLE TRANSACTIONS

Make multiple fund transfers to other financial institutions with only a minimal fee per transaction.

PESONet helps you do bulk payments to businesses or do transfers to individual accounts without going to the branch. Maximize your time through AUB’s Preferred Online and Mobile Banking.

1.What is PESONet?

PESONet (Philippine EFT System and Operations Network) is a fund transfer facility that allows you to make fund transfers of at least Php100, 000* per transaction to other financial institutions.

2. Where is PESONet available?

PESONet is available via AUB Preferred Online Banking and Mobile Banking. Currently unavailable via HelloMoney and Hello Pag-IBIG

3. How much is the Transfer Fee to other ewallets and banks?

Transfer fee for each transaction is Php 8. This fee is subject to change.

4. When will the funds be credited if sent through PESONet?

Crediting of funds to other ewallets and banks depends on the Receiving Bank’s two (2) cut-off times. If AUB is the Receiving Bank, crediting schedules will be: If fund transfers are made by 10AM, crediting will be by 12NN of the same banking day. If fund transfers are made by 4PM, crediting will be by 6PM of the same banking day. If fund transfers are made after the cut off times and during weekends and holidays, crediting will be on the following banking day.

5. How do I transfer funds via PESONet?

Simply log in to your Online or Mobile Banking account, choose Transfer Funds from the options then select PESONet. Input the Recipient’s Account Details and Amount to be transferred. Review the information then proceed. The Recipient will be able to receive the funds on the designated crediting schedule of his Bank.

6. Is PESONet fund transfer safe?

All fund transfers are fully secured using your username and password. Additional security for transactions above Php50, 000 with a Security Token Code.
