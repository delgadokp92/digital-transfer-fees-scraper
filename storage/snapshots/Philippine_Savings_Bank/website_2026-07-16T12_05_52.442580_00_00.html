Credit Mastercard

Credit Mastercard


  2. Other Products and Services
  3. PSBank Credit Mastercard

The PSBank Credit Mastercard is one simple card that takes care of your needs and helps you manage your expenses. A card you can enjoy for free, for life!

The PSBank Credit Mastercard is a card with no annual fee for life.  It allows you to pay for your simple needs and helps you manage your expenses. It offers flexible payment schemes, installment programs, and exclusive perks and privileges that give more value for your money.

Go to any PSBank branch or call our Customer Experience hotline to apply for a PSBank Credit Mastercard now!

 

 

The PSBank Credit Mastercard is a cross selling arrangement between Philippine Savings Bank and Metropolitan Bank and Trust Company.

Metropolitan Bank and Trust Company offers a range of credit card products suitable to meet the payment needs of the Philippine market. Cardholders can enjoy increased spending power and international purchasing convenience with acceptance in over 23 million establishments worldwide.

 



Product Features
  

  • No annual fee for life.
  • Chip card technology. The PSBank Credit Mastercard has an embedded chip that protects your credit card from fraud due to cloning – an occurrence when the personal and account information are copied and transferred to a fake card.
  • Value Added Services
    • Supplementary cards: Share your credit card benefits with loved ones while managing their spending.

    • Balance Transfer: Improve your cash flow by consolidating your card balances to the PSBank Credit Mastercard.  Easy installment terms at 6, 9, 12, 18 and 24 months.

    • Cash Rush: Cash advance when the need arises. Cash is received any time at any PSBank ATM or Metrobank ATM or any ATM with a Mastercard/Cirrus symbol, here and abroad.  You can call to inquire about the Cash Advance PIN at 88-700-772.

    • Convenient payment channels:
      • Branch Payments:  PSBank and Metrobank
      • PSBank Online and PSBank Mobile
      • For BancNet Member Bank Depositors: BancNet ATMs, BancNet Online
      • MegaLink ATMs

Foreign Exchange Transactions

All charges, advances or amount in currencies other than Philippine Peso (PhP) shall be converted to PhP. Transactions in US Dollar, Hong Kong Dollar, Japanese Yen, Euro, Singapore Dollar, Australian Dollar, British Pound, Canadian Dollar, Chinese Yuan, Swiss Francs, and Danish Kroner shall converted using the foreign exchange selling rate of Metrobank on transaction posting date. Indicative rates are published on www. metrobankcard.com. Transactions denominated in currencies other than the aforementioned shall be converted using Mastercard/Visa’s assessment fee plus 2.5% processing fee, the rate of which might be adjusted from time to time. The assessment fee shall likewise apply to transactions involving foreign currencies converted to PhP at point of sale, whether executed in the Philippines, abroad, or online. Service feed may also be charged to cover costs incurred to discharge the amount(s) due to Mastercard/Visa and /or the acquiring bank and/or merchant affiliates.

Truth and Transparency in Lending
  

Truth and Transparency in Lending
Enhanced Implementation of the Truth in Lending Act (Republic Act 3765)


The State protects its citizen from a lack of awareness of the true cost of credit to the customer by assuring a full disclosure of such cost and other terms and conditions with a view of preventing the uninformed use of credit.

As such, a disclosure statement is a required attachment to the loan contract. It shall include, at a minimum, the following information:

1.) Total amount to be financed
2.) Finance Charges and all other charges incident to the loan
3.) Net proceeds of the loan
4.) Schedule of Payments
5.) The percentage that the finance charge bears to the total amount to be financed expressed as an Effective Interest Rate (EIR)

Important Notice: The Bank is required to provide the borrower a copy of the disclosure statement prior to the consummation of the credit transaction.
